# Using foltone_lscustoms

## Overview

This script is a **standalone** mechanic menu. No job is required. Any player can access LS Customs points to customize, repair, and clean their vehicle.

---

## Vehicle Customization

### Accessing the Menu

1. Drive your vehicle to an LS Customs point
2. Enter the marked zone
3. The RageUI menu opens automatically

### Live Preview

All modifications are visible in real-time on your vehicle before confirming the purchase. Navigate through options to see the preview.

### Available Categories

- **Paint**: Primary, secondary, pearlescent, wheel color
- **Performance**: Engine, brakes, transmission, suspension, turbo
- **Aesthetic**: Spoiler, bumpers, skirts, exhaust, hood, roof, wheels, horn, windows, plates, xenon
- **Repair**: Restores the vehicle to full condition
- **Cleaning**: Removes dirt from the vehicle

### Confirming a Purchase

Select the desired modification and confirm. The amount is automatically deducted.

---

## NPC Towing Missions

1. Go to a towing point
2. Accept a mission from the menu
3. Follow the GPS to the broken-down vehicle
4. Tow the vehicle to the indicated garage
5. Receive your reward

---

## Invoice System

- Open the invoicing menu
- Enter the player ID and amount
- The player receives the invoice and can pay it
