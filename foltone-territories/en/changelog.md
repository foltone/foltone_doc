# Changelog - foltone_territories

## v1.0.0 - Initial Release

### Added
- Complete gang territory system
- Zone capture through NPC drug sales
- NPC reactions: accept, refuse, call police
- Better sale prices in captured territories
- Configurable zones (position, dimensions, rotation, required points)
- Configurable drugs (price, capture points, accept/reject rates)
- Blacklisted peds list
- Configurable police jobs for alerts
- Configurable gang jobs
- Database-saved capture progression
- RageUI menu for sales
- Multi-language support via translation files
- Compatible with es_extended and oxmysql
