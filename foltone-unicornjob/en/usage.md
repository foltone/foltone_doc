# Usage

## Overview

The Unicorn job allows players to manage the Unicorn nightclub. Includes bar service, an ingredient farming system, and a full boss menu for society management.

## Bar service

### Serving drinks

- Go behind the bar counter.
- Interact with the marker to open the service menu.
- Select the drink to prepare from the list.
- The drink is added to your inventory or served directly depending on configuration.

### How it works

The bartender can serve various drink types (beers, wines, cocktails, spirits). Each drink has a configurable price and requires the corresponding item to be available.

## Farming system

### Gathering ingredients

- Go to the configured farming points.
- Interact with the marker to gather ingredients (hops, grapes, etc.).
- Ingredients are added to your inventory.
- Use these ingredients to prepare drinks at the bar.

## Boss menu (RageUI)

Accessible only to the boss grade:

### Employee management
- **Hire**: approach a player and recruit them.
- **Fire**: select an employee from the list.
- **Promote/Demote**: change an employee's grade.

### Financial management
- View the society account balance.
- Deposit or withdraw money.
- Adjust salaries per grade.

### Outfit management
- Set the outfit for each grade.
- Employees change outfits at the locker.

## Society storage

- Go to the storage location.
- Deposit or withdraw items shared with coworkers.
- Useful for storing ingredients and prepared drinks.

## Locker

- Interact with the locker to put on your work outfit.
- Interact again to change back to civilian clothes.

## Garage

- Access the garage to take out a society vehicle.
- Available vehicles depend on your grade.
- Return the vehicle to the garage to store it.

## Tips

- Always keep a stock of ingredients to serve at the bar.
- Farming is accessible to all grades.
- Only the boss manages finances and personnel.
