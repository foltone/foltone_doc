# Usage — foltone_mechanic

## Vehicle customization

Bring a vehicle to a custom point, open the menu, choose mods/paint/neons/wheels, send bill to client. Client accepts (Y) or refuses (N).

## Tow missions

F6 menu > Toggle missions. Broken vehicles spawn on the map, tow with flatbed, deliver to depot. Random reward.

## Standard features

Boss, storage, locker, garage, duty, billing — same as all Foltone v2.0 jobs.
