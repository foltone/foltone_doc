# Configuration — foltone_mechanic

## General

```lua
Config.JobName = 'mechanic'
Config.SocietyName = 'society_mechanic'
Config.MenuSystem = 'rageui'   -- 'rageui' or 'ox_lib'
```

## Vehicle customization

Complete system: 6 paint categories, 30+ mod types, neons, xenon, wheels, window tint. Configurable pricing per mod, per vehicle class, or per model.

## Custom points

Multiple custom points, each restricted by job (e.g. mechanic, police).

## Tow missions

Random broken vehicle spawns, tow to delivery point, reward split between player and society.

## Discord Webhooks

All sensitive actions logged when configured.
